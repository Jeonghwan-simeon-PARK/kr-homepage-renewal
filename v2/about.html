HICARE.NET INC

Empowering US
RPM & CCM Services.

Based in California, Hicare.net Inc. is the United States subsidiary of HicareNet, dedicated to delivering advanced Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM) and Chronic Care Management (CCM) solutions.

Our Focus

We specialize in bridging clinical gaps with our comprehensive RPM and CCM platform, optimized for US healthcare providers to improve patient outcomes and operational efficiency.

Security & Standards

Unwavering commitment to data integrity. Our solutions are fully HIPAA compliant and certified under ISO 27001 (Information Security) and ISO 27701 (Privacy Information Management).

Global R&D Organization:
Full-Stack Innovation.

Our R&D organization is the backbone of HicareNet, driving full-stack service development from embedded hardware systems to cloud-native healthcare software. We don't just build apps; we architect entire care ecosystems.

Based strategically to provide 24/7 technical support for our US partners, our engineering teams ensure that the Hicare platform remains the most reliable and interoperable solution in the market, natively supporting HL7 FHIR protocols.
