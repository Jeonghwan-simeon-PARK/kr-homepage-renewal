COMMON QUESTIONS

Compliance FAQ

Can HicareNet sign a Business Associate Agreement (BAA)?
Yes. We provide a signed BAA to all covered entities based on the HHS model agreement, reviewed annually by our legal and compliance teams.
Can we review audit reports?
Upon signing an NDA, we can share our ISO 27001 and ISO 27701 certification reports, SOC 2 Type II report, and the most recent penetration test summary.
Where is patient data stored?
All patient data is stored within the United States on HIPAA-eligible cloud infrastructure with geo-redundant data centers. Data never leaves US jurisdiction.
How does HicareNet handle breach notification?
We commit to notifying affected covered entities within 24 hours of a confirmed PHI breach, with a detailed incident report and remediation steps.
