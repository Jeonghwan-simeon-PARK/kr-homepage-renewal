COMMON QUESTIONS

FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions about our RPM and CCM platform.

What devices are compatible with the Hicare Hub platform?
Hicare Hub supports a wide range of FDA-cleared Bluetooth and cellular-connected devices including blood pressure monitors, pulse oximeters, weight scales, glucometers, and more. Our platform integrates with 100+ device models across major manufacturers.
How quickly can we onboard a new practice?
Most practices are fully onboarded within 2–4 weeks. Our clinical implementation team handles device provisioning, EHR integration, staff training, and patient enrollment workflows.
Does HicareNet support HL7 FHIR integration?
Yes. Our platform natively supports HL7 FHIR R4, enabling bi-directional data exchange with major EHR systems including Epic, Cerner, and athenahealth. FHIR-based EMR integration for the full suite is planned for H1 2026.
What languages does the patient app support?
The Hicare patient app currently supports 7 languages: English, Spanish, Korean, Chinese (Simplified), Vietnamese, Tagalog, and Portuguese — addressing the majority of US multilingual healthcare populations.
