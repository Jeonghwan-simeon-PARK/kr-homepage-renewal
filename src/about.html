Our Story

Our Journey

2000s

Founded as InSung Information

With over 20 years of healthcare IT experience in South Korea, InSung Information established itself as a trusted leader in hospital information systems and clinical workflow solutions.

2020

Evolution into HicareNet

Recognizing the growing demand for connected healthcare in the United States, we launched HicareNet to bring our proven technology and clinical expertise to the American healthcare market.

2022

Expanded US Operations

Opened our engineering office in San Jose and expanded our Irvine headquarters to serve the growing demand for comprehensive RPM, CCM, and AWV solutions across the country.

2026

Serving 200+ Clinics Nationwide

Today, HicareNet serves over 200 contracted clinics and 15,000+ active patients across the United States, with FHIR-based EMR integration on the horizon.
