Our Story

Our Journey

2000s

InSung Info Healthcare Division Heritage

Built on 20+ years of healthcare IT expertise from InSung Info's healthcare division. Developed deep capabilities in patient vital sign monitoring, chronic disease management, and non-face-to-face diagnosis/monitoring technology.

2020

HicareNet Established as Independent Corporation

In 2020, HicareNet was established as an independent corporation through physical division from InSung Info's healthcare business division. The goal: global commercialization of the digital healthcare platform built on 20+ years of accumulated technology.

2021

US Subsidiary & VA Project Deployment

In 2021, HicareNet established its US subsidiary. The Hicare Hub gateway, supporting 120+ medical devices, was deployed in US VA (Veterans Affairs) healthcare projects with 10,000+ units exported. Additional gateway exports: 1,000 units to Italy, 3,000 units to France.

2023

RPM/CCM Service Launch & Growth

RPM/CCM service launched in April 2023, starting with 15 hospitals and 1,000 patients in California. By 2024, expanded to 37 hospitals and 2,000+ patients. In 2025, serving 40+ hospitals with 3,000+ enrolled patients and 300+ monthly insurance claims.
