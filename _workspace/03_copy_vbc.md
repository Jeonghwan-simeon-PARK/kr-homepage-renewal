## Service Description

- **lead_en**: "Value-Based Care is not a separate service -- it is the outcome of everything your practice does with RPM, CCM, APCM, and AWV data. HicareNet connects the dots: every blood pressure reading, every care plan update, and every wellness visit feeds directly into your quality scores and financial performance."
- **lead_ko**: "가치기반진료(VBC)는 별도의 서비스가 아니라, RPM, CCM, APCM, AWV 데이터가 만들어내는 결과입니다. 하이케어넷이 점을 연결합니다: 모든 혈압 측정값, 모든 케어 플랜 업데이트, 모든 건강검진이 품질 점수와 재무 성과에 직접 반영됩니다."

- **key_points_en**:
  1. Dual revenue strategy -- earn immediate FFS reimbursement from RPM/CCM/APCM while building toward shared savings and MIPS bonuses
  2. Automatic quality measure mapping -- RPM blood pressure data maps to HEDIS Controlling High Blood Pressure; AWV completion maps to Stars preventive measures
  3. Real-time quality dashboards -- track MIPS scores, HEDIS gaps-in-care, and Stars performance as data flows in
  4. Multilingual care improves health equity -- meet ACO REACH equity requirements and improve CAHPS patient experience scores
  5. VBC readiness for smaller practices -- support for practices that lack population health infrastructure to participate in ACO and MIPS programs

  3. 실시간 품질 대시보드 -- 데이터가 유입되는 대로 MIPS 점수, HEDIS 케어 갭, Stars 성과를 추적합니다
  4. 다국어 케어가 건강 형평성 향상 -- ACO REACH 형평성 요건 충족과 CAHPS 환자 경험 점수 개선에 기여합니다

## CPT Codes Display

- **codes**: VBC is a payment framework, not a billable service with its own CPT codes. VBC performance is driven by the following HicareNet services:

  | Service | Codes | VBC Contribution |
  |---------|-------|------------------|
  | RPM | 99453-99458 | Blood pressure/glucose control (HEDIS); readmission reduction (cost) |
  | CCM | 99490-99489 | Chronic condition management (quality); care coordination (cost) |
  | APCM | G0556/G0557 | Comprehensive management (quality + cost) |
  | AWV | G0438/G0439 | Preventive service completion (Stars/HEDIS) |
  | HRA | 96160 | Risk identification and gap-in-care closure |

- **billing_note_en**: "VBC does not have its own billing codes. Revenue comes from two streams: (1) FFS reimbursement through RPM/CCM/APCM/AWV codes and (2) VBC incentive payments such as MIPS bonuses (+/-9%), MSSP shared savings (40-75%), and MA Stars quality bonuses."
- **billing_note_ko**: "VBC는 자체 청구 코드가 없습니다. 수익은 두 가지 흐름으로 발생합니다: (1) RPM/CCM/APCM/AWV 코드를 통한 행위별 수가 및 (2) MIPS 보너스(+/-9%), MSSP 공유 절감액(40-75%), MA Stars 품질 보너스 등 VBC 인센티브입니다."
