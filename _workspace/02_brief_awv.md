# AWV (Annual Wellness Visit) Content Brief

## Executive Summary

AWV is a Medicare preventive service providing ~$118-175 per visit in direct revenue, but its strategic value far exceeds the single-visit reimbursement. AWV serves as the **"gateway" to the entire care management pipeline** -- discovering patients for RPM, CCM, and APCM enrollment. The AWV page should be positioned as the starting point of a revenue journey, where one AWV visit can unlock $2,500+ in annual per-patient revenue through downstream service enrollment. HicareNet's multilingual digital HRA, pre-visit distribution, and AI-powered patient identification make AWV a natural extension of the platform.

## CMS/Regulatory Foundation

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Program | Medicare Annual Wellness Visit |
| Authority | ACA Section 4103, 42 CFR 410.15 |
| Effective Since | January 1, 2011 |
| Patient Cost | None (ACA preventive service -- deductible/coinsurance waived) |
| Frequency | Annually (12+ months since last AWV or IPPE) |

### HCPCS Codes

| Code | Description | CY2025 Rate (NF) |
|------|------------|-------------------|
| G0438 | Initial AWV (first AWV ever) | [UNVERIFIED] ~$175.07 |
| G0439 | Subsequent AWV (annual) | [UNVERIFIED] ~$118.80 |
| G0402 | IPPE ("Welcome to Medicare" visit, lifetime once) | [UNVERIFIED] ~$173.65 |

### Required AWV Components
1. Health Risk Assessment (HRA) -- **required**
2. Medical/Family History -- collect (initial) or update (subsequent)
3. Height, Weight, BMI, Blood Pressure
4. Cognitive Assessment
5. Depression Screening (PHQ-2 → PHQ-9)
6. Functional Ability / Safety Assessment (falls risk)
7. Current Providers/Suppliers list
8. Personalized Prevention Plan of Services (PPPS)

### Key Billing Rules
- Patient pays nothing (ACA preventive service)
- Modifier 25 on E/M code if separate problem addressed same day
- Can serve as CCM/APCM initiating visit
- 12-month interval between AWV visits
- AWV + E/M same day billing is allowed (with Modifier 25)

## Clinical Workflow

1. **Patient Identification & Scheduling** -- EHR-based recall for AWV-eligible patients, distributed scheduling throughout year
2. **Pre-Visit Preparation** -- Digital HRA distribution via patient app, medical record review, screening checklist preparation
3. **Day-of-Visit Services** -- HRA completion/review, measurements, cognitive/depression screening, functional assessment, PPPS creation
4. **Prevention Plan (PPPS)** -- Recommended preventive services schedule, risk factor interventions, RPM/CCM/APCM enrollment
5. **Documentation & Billing** -- All required components documented, G0438/G0439 claim, Modifier 25 if same-day E/M
