# VBC (Value-Based Care) Content Brief

## Executive Summary

VBC is not a billable service with a CPT code -- it is a strategic framework under which all other HicareNet services (RPM, CCM, APCM, AWV, HRA) contribute to organizational performance goals. The VBC page should be positioned as the "why it all matters" story: how HicareNet's platform drives quality measure improvement (HEDIS/Stars), cost reduction (readmission decrease), and revenue maximization (shared savings + FFS billing). The key messaging innovation is the **"dual revenue model"** -- earn FFS reimbursement (RPM/CCM/APCM codes) while simultaneously improving VBC incentives (MIPS bonus, shared savings, Stars bonus).

## CMS/Regulatory Foundation

VBC is a payment model framework, not a single CMS program. Key VBC models:

| Model | Description | Risk Level | Participation |
|-------|------------|-----------|---------------|
| MIPS | Performance-based +/-9% Medicare adjustment | Low | ~700,000+ providers [ESTIMATED] |
| MSSP | ACO shared savings (40-75%) | Medium-High | ~500+ ACOs, ~11M beneficiaries [VERIFIED] |
| ACO REACH | Equity-focused total cost of care | High | ~100+ organizations [ESTIMATED] |
| MCP (Making Care Primary) | 10-year voluntary primary care model | Progressive | Launching [VERIFIED] |
| MA Stars | Quality bonus payments for 4+ star plans | Medium | ~72% of plans at 4+ stars [ESTIMATED] |

### HicareNet VBC Differentiators
1. **Dual revenue model** -- FFS billing (RPM/CCM/APCM codes) + VBC incentives (shared savings, MIPS bonus)
2. **Integrated platform** -- RPM + CCM + APCM + AWV + HRA data feeds automatically into VBC quality measures
3. **Multilingual patient engagement** -- improves CAHPS patient experience scores and health equity metrics
4. **Small clinic focus** -- VBC consulting/support for practices that lack population health infrastructure
5. **ISO dual certification** -- meets quality/security requirements for ACO participation
