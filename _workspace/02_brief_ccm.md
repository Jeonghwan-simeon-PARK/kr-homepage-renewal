# CCM (Chronic Care Management) Content Brief

## Executive Summary

CCM is HicareNet's second core service, providing non-face-to-face care management for Medicare patients with 2+ chronic conditions. The US CCM market is valued at $2.2B (2024) with projected growth to $18.8B by 2034 (CAGR 11.7%) [REPORTED - Tier 3]. A critical narrative shift is needed: CCM must be positioned alongside APCM as CMS transitions toward simplified care management billing. HicareNet's multilingual capability is the single most important differentiator for CCM, as care coordination requires extensive patient communication -- a task made difficult by language barriers in diverse communities.

## CMS/Regulatory Foundation

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Program | Chronic Care Management Services |
| CMS Category | Care Management Services |
| Effective Since | January 1, 2015 (CY2015 PFS Final Rule) |
| Eligibility | Medicare Part B, 2+ chronic conditions (expected to last 12+ months) |
| CY2025 Status | Active but in transition period with APCM (G0556/G0557). Cannot bill CCM and APCM for same patient in same month. |

### CPT/HCPCS Codes

| Code | Description | Time Requirement | CY2025 Rate (NF) |
|------|------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| 99490 | Basic CCM, clinical staff time | 20+ min/month | [UNVERIFIED] ~$64.02 |
| 99491 | CCM by physician/QHP directly | 30+ min/month | [UNVERIFIED] ~$87.67 |
| 99487 | Complex CCM, clinical staff time | 60+ min/month | [UNVERIFIED] ~$130.41 |
| 99489 | Complex CCM add-on (with 99487) | Additional 30+ min | [UNVERIFIED] ~$63.43 |

**Monthly per-patient scenarios**: $64 (basic) to $194 (complex + add-on) [UNVERIFIED]

### Key Billing Rules
- Minute-by-minute time tracking required (biggest provider pain point)
- 24/7 patient access to care team required
- Electronic care plan (e-care plan) must be stored in EHR
- Cannot bill simultaneously with APCM (G0556) or PCM (99424-99427)
- CAN bill simultaneously with RPM (99453-99458)

## Clinical Workflow

1. **Patient Identification** -- 2+ chronic conditions (ICD-10), Medicare Part B, no other provider billing CCM
2. **Comprehensive Care Plan** -- Problem list, medication list, condition-specific goals, specialist referrals, SDOH resources
3. **Monthly Non-Face-to-Face Services** -- Phone/messaging/remote monitoring, medication management, patient education, specialist coordination, time tracking (20/30/60 min thresholds)
4. **Care Plan Review** -- Annual comprehensive review minimum, update on status changes
5. **Documentation & Billing** -- Service content, time spent (minute-level), threshold verification, code selection

### HicareNet Competitive Advantages (CCM-specific)
1. **Multilingual care coordination (EN/KR/ES)** -- THE differentiator. No competitor markets multilingual CCM.
2. **AI-powered time tracking automation** -- Reduces the #1 CCM pain point
3. **RPM + CCM simultaneous billing** -- Unified platform maximizes per-patient revenue
4. **Multilingual consent templates** -- Korean/Spanish consent forms reduce enrollment friction
5. **APCM transition support** -- Help existing CCM customers evaluate and transition to APCM

### Competitive Gaps
- ThoroughCare already supports APCM; HicareNet does not yet
- Case studies showing CCM outcomes are lacking
- 24/7 access coverage model not clearly marketed
