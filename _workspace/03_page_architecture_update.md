## 2. Solutions Page -- Service Order

### Before: RPM > CCM > AWV > VBC > FHIR
### After: RPM > CCM > APCM > AWV > HRA > VBC > FHIR

Rationale:
- RPM and CCM remain first (flagship services, highest revenue)
- APCM follows CCM (natural progression, CCM-to-APCM transition narrative)
- AWV follows APCM (gateway service that feeds RPM/CCM/APCM)
- HRA follows AWV (sub-service of AWV, Patient Discovery Engine)
- VBC comes last (strategic framework that ties everything together)
- FHIR remains at end (coming soon, enabler not service)
