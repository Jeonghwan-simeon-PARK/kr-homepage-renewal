## CRITICAL Gaps

### GAP-C1: APCM Service Completely Missing
- **Current State**: Zero content in services.json, navigation.json, en.json, or ko.json
- **Research Finding**: APCM launched January 1, 2025 (CMS CY2025 PFS). G0556/G0557 codes active. Expands eligible population from CCM's 2+ conditions to APCM's 1+ condition. Eliminates time tracking burden.
- **Competitor Status**: ThoroughCare already has APCM support and dedicated content page. All other competitors in "unknown" state.
- **Impact**: HicareNet is marked "X" for APCM in competitor feature matrix. Competitors will fill this gap soon.
- **Recommendation**: Create complete APCM service entry (services.json), navigation item, i18n keys, and dedicated content section. This is the #1 priority for the homepage renewal.

### GAP-C2: CTA Pattern Misaligned with Industry Standard
- **Current State**: Primary CTA is "Contact Us" / "Get Started" across the site
- **Research Finding**: 7 out of 8 analyzed competitors use "Book a Demo" / "Schedule a Demo" / "Request a Demo" as primary CTA
- **Impact**: "Contact Us" has the highest conversion barrier of all CTA types. Likely reducing qualified lead generation.
- **Recommendation**: Change primary CTA to "Book a Demo" site-wide. Add secondary CTAs: "Calculate Your ROI", "Download Billing Guide"

### GAP-C3: Messaging Level at 1-2 (Feature/Solution), Industry Leaders at 3-4 (Outcome/Audience)
- **Current State**: Hero says "Advanced Remote Patient Monitoring" (feature description). Features like "120+ medical device types supported via Hicare Hub IoT gateway" lead with technology specs.
- **Research Finding**: High-performing competitors (Vivify, HRS) lead with outcomes ("Reduce readmissions", "Drive better outcomes"). Vivify uses audience-first navigation ("Who We Serve").
- **Impact**: Feature-first messaging speaks to IT evaluators, not to the decision-makers (practice managers, physicians) who authorize purchases.
- **Recommendation**: Rewrite all hero headlines to outcome-first language. Add "Who We Help" navigation section. Lead with results, support with technology.

## HIGH Gaps

### GAP-H1: HRA Not Recognized as Independent Service
- **Current**: AWV feature1 mentions "Automated Health Risk Assessments (HRA)" as one of four AWV features
- **Research**: HRA is a strategic "Patient Discovery Engine" that feeds RPM/CCM/APCM enrollment pipelines. CPT 96160/96161 exist for standalone billing. Multiple competitors (Prevounce, WellTrackONE) specialize in HRA/AWV.
- **Recommendation**: Elevate HRA to visible service level. Create combined "AWV & HRA" page with HRA having a prominent section. Add HRA to services.json.

### GAP-H2: No Billing Detail on Solution Pages
- **Current**: CPT codes listed in services.json but no rate information, billing rules, time requirements, or revenue calculations anywhere in en.json/ko.json
- **Research**: ThoroughCare and ChronicCareIQ publish detailed billing guides as educational content. These drive significant SEO traffic.
- **Recommendation**: Add billing code tables with rates (noted as estimates), billing rules, and monthly revenue scenarios to each solution page. Create separate "Billing Guides" resource section.

### GAP-H3: No Case Studies or Evidence-Based Content
- **Current**: No case studies, no clinical evidence citations, no ROI data on the website
- **Research**: HRS claims "50% readmission reduction" with case studies. HealthSnap has interactive ROI calculator. ThoroughCare publishes extensive case study library.
- **Recommendation**: Priority: Collect 3-5 case studies from California clinics. Cite verified clinical evidence (TIM-HF2 Lancet, Margolis JAMA). Build ROI calculator (mid-term).

### GAP-H4: AWV Missing IPPE Code and Component Detail
- **Current**: services.json has G0438/G0439 only. en.json mentions AWV but no required component checklist.
- **Research**: G0402 (IPPE) is missing. AWV has 8 required components (HRA, cognitive assessment, depression screening, functional assessment, PPPS, etc.) that differentiate it from a regular physical.
- **Recommendation**: Add G0402 to AWV codes. Add required components detail. Explain AWV as revenue gateway.

### GAP-H5: No "Who We Help" Audience-First Navigation
- **Current**: Navigation is Solutions | About | Resources | Compliance | Contact
- **Research**: Vivify Health uses "Who We Serve" as a primary navigation item. HRS uses "Who We Help". This is the Level 4 (highest) messaging maturity pattern.
- **Recommendation**: Add "Who We Help" nav section with segments: Small Clinics, Community Health Centers, FQHC, VA & Government.

### GAP-H6: Multilingual Advantage Underutilized in Messaging
- **Current**: Multilingual mentioned as CCM feature2 ("Multilingual care coordination (English, Korean, Spanish)"). Not prominently featured elsewhere.
- **Research**: ZERO competitors market multilingual RPM/CCM. This is HicareNet's most unique differentiator. Competitor-analyst rates it 5/5 stars for differentiation strength.
- **Recommendation**: Elevate multilingual to a primary message pillar across ALL solution pages. Consider dedicated "Multilingual Care" page or section. Position as "Inclusive Care for Diverse Communities."

### GAP-H7: No Resource/Education Content Strategy
- **Current**: Resources page has Blog and FAQ only
- **Research**: ThoroughCare publishes billing guides, program explanations, webinars. HRS has case studies, white papers. HealthSnap has ROI calculator. These drive organic traffic and lead nurturing.
- **Recommendation**: Expand Resources with: Billing Guides (RPM/CCM/APCM/AWV), Case Studies, Device Catalog, and eventually ROI Calculator and Webinars.

### GAP-H8: VBC Page Lacks Actionable Framework
- **Current**: VBC in services.json has no CPT codes (correct), but en.json content is generic: "Quality measure dashboards", "HEDIS/Stars rating optimization"
- **Research**: VBC encompasses MIPS (+/-9%), MSSP (shared savings 40-75%), ACO REACH, Stars. The "dual revenue model" (FFS billing + VBC incentives) is the compelling story for small clinics.
- **Recommendation**: Restructure VBC page around the dual revenue narrative. Show specific quality measure mappings. Explain MIPS/MSSP/Stars in simple terms.

## MEDIUM Gaps

### GAP-M1: CCM Page Missing APCM Transition Narrative
- **Current**: CCM page makes no mention of APCM
- **Research**: CMS launched APCM specifically to address CCM's low adoption (5-8% of eligible). APCM cannot be billed with CCM for same patient. CCM's future is uncertain.
- **Recommendation**: Add CCM vs APCM comparison section. Help providers decide when to stay with CCM vs transition.

### GAP-M2: ISO Dual Certification Underemphasized
- **Current**: ISO 27001 and ISO 27701 mentioned in compliance page only
- **Research**: Most competitors have only HIPAA + SOC2. ISO dual certification is a rare differentiator rated 4/5 stars by competitor-analyst.
- **Recommendation**: Add ISO trust badges to every page footer. Create "Beyond HIPAA" messaging for security-conscious buyers.

### GAP-M3: FHIR Page is Roadmap-Only
- **Current**: FHIR page shows development timeline (Q1/Q2/H2 2026) but no regulatory context
- **Research**: 21st Century Cures Act, Information Blocking rules, USCDI v3, and TEFCA create a regulatory mandate for FHIR. 96%+ of certified EHRs support FHIR R4.
- **Recommendation**: Add regulatory context (Cures Act, Information Blocking). Show EHR partner compatibility. Explain bidirectional data exchange value.

### GAP-M4: No RPM Revenue/ROI Messaging
- **Current**: RPM outcome3 says "Generate new revenue streams with CPT 99453-99458" but no dollar amounts
- **Research**: RPM can generate ~$147/patient/month or ~$1,764/year. RPM+CCM combination yields ~$160-200/patient/month. This is the most compelling ROI story for practice managers.
- **Recommendation**: Add specific revenue projections (with [ESTIMATED] disclaimers) to RPM and CCM pages.

### GAP-M5: 120+ Device Advantage Not Visually Demonstrated
- **Current**: "120+ medical device types" text mentioned but no device catalog, search, or visual representation
- **Research**: CareSimple provides FDA-cleared device lists. No competitor offers 120+ device breadth.
- **Recommendation**: Create device catalog page (or section) with device categories, brands, and compatibility information. Mid-term priority.

### GAP-M6: Gateway Hardware Story Undertold
- **Current**: HH-800A and HH-930 described in technology section but not connected to RPM value
- **Research**: No competitor has self-manufactured gateway/hub hardware. VA 10,000+ export is a powerful trust signal.
- **Recommendation**: Connect gateway story to RPM reliability narrative. "Proven at Scale: 10,000+ Gateways Deployed to US VA."

### GAP-M7: Korean Content (ko.json) Likely Missing Many New Keys
- **Current**: ko.json has parity with en.json for existing content
- **Research**: ~85 new i18n keys needed for APCM, HRA elevation, billing details, navigation updates, and cross-service linkages
- **Recommendation**: All new en.json keys must have ko.json counterparts. Korean translations need cultural adaptation, not just translation.
