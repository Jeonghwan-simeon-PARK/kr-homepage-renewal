## CPT Codes Display

- **codes**:
  | Code | Description | Frequency |
  |------|-------------|-----------|
  | G0438 | Initial AWV (first AWV ever for patient) | Once per patient |
  | G0439 | Subsequent AWV (annual) | Every 12+ months |
  | G0402 | IPPE ("Welcome to Medicare" visit) | Once per lifetime |

- **billing_note_en**: "Patients pay nothing for AWV (ACA preventive service). Can serve as CCM/APCM initiating visit. Same-day E/M billing allowed with Modifier 25 for separate clinical problems. Minimum 12-month interval between AWVs."
- **billing_note_ko**: "AWV는 ACA 예방 서비스로 환자 부담금이 없습니다. CCM/APCM 개시 방문으로 활용 가능합니다. 별도 임상 문제에 대해 Modifier 25로 같은 날 E/M 청구가 가능합니다. AWV 간 최소 12개월 간격이 필요합니다."

## Required AWV Components (visual checklist)

1. Health Risk Assessment (HRA)
2. Medical/Family History
3. Height, Weight, BMI, Blood Pressure
4. Cognitive Assessment
5. Depression Screening (PHQ-2 / PHQ-9)
6. Functional Ability / Safety Assessment
7. Current Providers/Suppliers List
8. Personalized Prevention Plan of Services (PPPS)
