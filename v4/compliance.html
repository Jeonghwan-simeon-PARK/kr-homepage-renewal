Compliance & Certifications

Our commitment to security, privacy, and regulatory compliance.

At HicareNet, security and compliance are not afterthoughts -- they are foundational to everything we build. As a healthcare technology company managing sensitive patient data across our RPM, CCM, AWV, and Value-Based Care platforms, we maintain rigorous compliance with international security standards and U.S. healthcare regulations.

Certified

ISO 27001:2022 -- Information Security Management System

HicareNet holds ISO 27001:2022 certification for our Information Security Management System (ISMS), the internationally recognized standard for managing information security.

  • Scope: Covers all information assets related to our connected care management platform, including cloud infrastructure, application systems, and operational processes.
  • Annual Audit: Our ISMS is independently audited annually by an accredited certification body to ensure ongoing conformity with the standard.
  • Risk Assessment: We conduct systematic risk assessments to identify, analyze, and evaluate information security risks, implementing appropriate controls to mitigate identified threats.
  • Physical & Technical Controls: Comprehensive controls spanning access management, cryptography, physical security, operations security, communications security, and incident management.
Certified

ISO 27701:2019 -- Privacy Information Management System

Our ISO 27701:2019 certification extends our ISMS to establish a Privacy Information Management System (PIMS), providing a framework for managing personal data in compliance with global privacy regulations.

  • Privacy Extension: Builds upon our ISO 27001 foundation to add privacy-specific requirements for processing personally identifiable information (PII).
  • GDPR & CCPA Alignment: Our PIMS is designed to support compliance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other applicable privacy laws.
  • Privacy-by-Design: Privacy considerations are embedded into the design and development of all our products and services from the earliest stages.
  • Data Subject Rights: Established processes for handling data subject access requests, correction requests, deletion requests, and other privacy rights exercises.
Compliant

HIPAA -- Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act

HicareNet maintains full compliance with HIPAA regulations as a Business Associate to healthcare providers (Covered Entities) across the United States.

  • Privacy Rule: We implement administrative, physical, and technical safeguards to protect the privacy of Protected Health Information (PHI) in accordance with 45 CFR Part 160 and Subparts A and E of Part 164.
  • Security Rule: Our platform meets all required and addressable implementation specifications of the HIPAA Security Rule (45 CFR Part 160 and Subparts A and C of Part 164) for electronic PHI (ePHI).
  • Breach Notification Rule: We maintain documented breach notification procedures to ensure timely notification to Covered Entities, affected individuals, and the HHS Secretary as required by the Breach Notification Rule.
  • Business Associate Agreements: We execute comprehensive BAAs with all Covered Entity clients, clearly defining the permitted uses and disclosures of PHI, security obligations, and breach notification requirements.
  • Annual Training: All HicareNet employees and contractors complete mandatory HIPAA training upon hire and annually thereafter, with documented attestation of compliance.
Security Controls

Technical Security Controls

Beyond our certifications, HicareNet implements a defense-in-depth security architecture with multiple layers of technical controls:

  • AES-256 Encryption: All data at rest is encrypted using AES-256, the highest commercially available encryption standard. Data in transit is protected using TLS 1.3 with forward secrecy.
  • TLS 1.3: All communications between clients, our platform, and integrated systems are encrypted using TLS 1.3, ensuring the highest level of transport security.
  • RBAC + MFA: Role-Based Access Control ensures users access only the data and functions necessary for their role. Multi-Factor Authentication is mandatory for all administrative and clinical access.
  • Web Application Firewall (WAF): Our cloud infrastructure is protected by enterprise-grade WAF rules that detect and block common web exploits, SQL injection, cross-site scripting, and other OWASP Top 10 threats.
  • 24/7 SIEM Monitoring: Our Security Information and Event Management system provides continuous real-time monitoring, automated threat detection, and rapid incident response across all systems.
  • Quarterly Penetration Testing: Independent third-party security firms conduct quarterly penetration tests of our platform, with all findings remediated according to risk-based timelines.
