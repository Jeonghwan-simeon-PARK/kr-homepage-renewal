2. Description of Services

HicareNet provides technology-enabled connected care management solutions for healthcare providers, including but not limited to:

  • Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM): Real-time collection, transmission, and analysis of patient health data from FDA-cleared monitoring devices, supporting over 120 compatible device types including blood pressure monitors, glucometers, pulse oximeters, weight scales, and thermometers.
  • Chronic Care Management (CCM): Comprehensive care coordination platform for managing patients with two or more chronic conditions, including care plan development, monthly patient outreach, and clinical documentation.
  • Annual Wellness Visits (AWV): Digital tools and workflows for conducting Initial Preventive Physical Examinations (IPPE) and subsequent Annual Wellness Visits, including Health Risk Assessments and personalized prevention plans.
  • Value-Based Care (VBC): Analytics, population health management tools, and quality measure reporting to support healthcare organizations transitioning to value-based payment models.
  • FHIR Integration: HL7 FHIR R4-compliant APIs and integration services for seamless data exchange with Electronic Medical Record (EMR) systems, Health Information Exchanges (HIEs), and other healthcare IT platforms (anticipated H1 2026).
