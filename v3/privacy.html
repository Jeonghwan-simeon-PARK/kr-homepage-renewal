Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025 · Last Updated: March 15, 2025

1. Information We Collect

HicareNet, Inc. ("HicareNet," "we," "us," or "our"), a subsidiary of Hicare Co., Ltd., collects information necessary to deliver our healthcare technology services, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, professional credentials, and organizational affiliation.
  • Protected Health Information (PHI): Patient demographic information, clinical data including vital signs and biometric readings, as required for RPM and CCM services.
  • Usage Data: Device information, browser type, IP address, platform usage patterns, and session data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining our RPM, CCM, and care management platform services
  • Processing and transmitting clinical data between healthcare providers and patients
  • Generating care plans, clinical reports, and compliance documentation
  • Improving platform performance and developing new features
  • Sending service-related notifications and responding to support requests

3. HIPAA Compliance

As a healthcare technology provider, HicareNet operates in full compliance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) and the HITECH Act. All PHI is handled in accordance with HIPAA Privacy, Security, and Breach Notification Rules. We execute Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) with all covered entities before processing any PHI.

4. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures backed by ISO 27001 and ISO 27701 certifications:

  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  • Role-based access control with mandatory multi-factor authentication
  • 24/7 security monitoring with intrusion detection
  • Regular third-party penetration testing and vulnerability assessments

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information or PHI. Information may be shared with authorized healthcare providers, vetted third-party service providers bound by BAAs, or as required by applicable law.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at info@hicare.net.
