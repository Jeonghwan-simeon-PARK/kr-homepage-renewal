Compliance FAQ

How does HicareNet ensure HIPAA compliance?
We implement comprehensive administrative, physical, and technical safeguards as required by HIPAA. This includes encrypted data storage and transmission, role-based access controls, regular risk assessments, Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) with all partners, and mandatory staff training on privacy practices.
What data is collected from patients?
We collect vital signs data transmitted by compatible Bluetooth-enabled monitoring devices, including blood pressure, blood glucose, pulse oximetry, weight, and temperature. All data is collected with patient consent and handled in accordance with our privacy policy and applicable regulations.
Where is patient data stored?
All patient data is stored in HIPAA-compliant, SOC 2 Type II certified cloud infrastructure within the United States. We use geo-redundant storage with automatic failover to ensure 99.99% uptime and data durability.
How can I request a security audit or BAA?
Contact our compliance team at compliance@hicare.net or call 02-3400-7247. We provide Business Associate Agreements to all covered entities, and our security documentation is available upon request under NDA.
