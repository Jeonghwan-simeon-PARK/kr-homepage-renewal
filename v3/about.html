About HicareNet

20+ Years of Healthcare Innovation

Our Mission

To make remote patient monitoring accessible, affordable, and effective for every healthcare provider in the United States. We believe that continuous, connected care reduces hospital readmissions and improves patient outcomes.

Our Vision

A world where every patient with a chronic condition is continuously monitored, intelligently alerted, and proactively cared for, regardless of language, location, or technical literacy.

Our Journey

2020

HicareNet Established

Founded as an independent corporation, spinning off from InSung Info's healthcare division with 20+ years of technology heritage.

2021

US Subsidiary Established

Launched HicareNet Inc. in California to serve the US healthcare market with RPM and CCM solutions.

2023

RPM/CCM Service Launch

Launched RPM/CCM services in April 2023, onboarding 15 hospitals and 1,000 patients in California.

2024

Rapid Growth & Global Expansion

Scaled to 37 hospitals and 2,000+ patients. Achieved ISO 27701 certification, launched AI-powered clinical platform, and expanded to Italy and France.

2025

40+ Hospitals & 3,000+ Patients

Reached 40 contracted hospitals, 3,000+ enrolled patients, and 300+ monthly claims across California.
