Frequently Asked Questions

What is Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM)?

RPM uses connected medical devices to collect patient health data outside of traditional healthcare settings. Providers can monitor vitals like blood pressure, blood glucose, weight, and oxygen saturation in real time, enabling proactive interventions and reducing hospital readmissions.

How does HicareNet handle HIPAA compliance?

HicareNet is built with HIPAA compliance at its core. We employ end-to-end encryption, role-based access controls, comprehensive audit logging, and maintain ISO 27001 and ISO 27701 certifications. All data is stored in SOC 2-compliant data centers within the United States.

What devices are compatible with the HicareNet platform?

Our platform supports 120+ Bluetooth-enabled medical devices including blood pressure monitors, glucometers, pulse oximeters, weight scales, and thermometers. All devices are plug-and-play with voice-guided setup instructions for patients.

How do providers get reimbursed for RPM and CCM services?

HicareNet automates CPT code generation and Medicare billing reports. Providers can bill under CPT codes 99453, 99454, 99457, and 99458 for RPM, and 99490/99491 for CCM. Our platform tracks patient interaction time and generates compliant documentation with a single click.
